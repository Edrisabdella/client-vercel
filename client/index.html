Problem Statement

Education inequality remains one of the most significant challenges to sustainable development worldwide

Limited Access

Over 260 million children and youth worldwide do not attend school, and many more lack access to quality education resources.

Digital Divide

The COVID-19 pandemic highlighted the digital divide, with nearly 50% of students in developing countries lacking internet access.

Lack of Support

Many students struggle academically without access to personalized support, tutoring, or mentorship opportunities.

About OpenLearn

Our mission to bridge educational gaps through technology

Our Story

OpenLearn was founded in 2025 by Edris Abdella Nuure with a vision to create an inclusive digital education platform that bridges educational gaps worldwide. Inspired by the United Nations Sustainable Development Goal 4: Quality Education, our platform leverages technology to make learning accessible to everyone, regardless of their location or socioeconomic status.

We believe that education is a fundamental human right and that technology can be a powerful equalizer. Our peer-to-peer model connects learners with tutors, creates collaborative learning communities, and provides free access to high-quality educational resources.

Today, OpenLearn has impacted thousands of students across multiple countries, with a growing community of educators, volunteers, and partners who share our vision of making quality education accessible to all.

Our Values

We believe education should be accessible to everyone, regardless of background or location.
